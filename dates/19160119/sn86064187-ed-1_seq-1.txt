Thr following note, signed "J. O." was found by Jus
tice of the Peace Donnell near the body of Welday:
"I have not slept for weeks. Madness was lurking
just around the corner so I'm going on the long journey
"The pity of it that so many heartstrings are bound
about each of us it makes it hard to go. J. O.
"Notify Mrs. F.. M. Welday, 1030 Ninth street. Lo
rain, Ohio."
Justice of the Peace Donnell also found two letters,
sealed. One was addressed to "W. O. Moore, care high
school Personal," and the other, "II. M. Sinclair, care
Scott-Thompson building, city."
A package of papers, believed to include insurance
documents, addressed to "John M. Hale, care Wilkin-Hale
bank," was also found in the room.
Justice Donnell refused to open the letters or pack
age, saying that was a duty of the coroner's jury.
