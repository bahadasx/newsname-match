Administration Considers
Asking Permission to Send
Troops to Wipe Out Villa
(BY KVLNINO IIEBALD LEASED WIRE)
Washington, March 9. Secretary Lansing late to
day announced that the United States was considering
asking General Carranza for permission to send Amer
ican troops into Mexico to capture the Villa bandits
who raided Columbus, N. M.
Secretary Lansing said that if permisjion were ask
ed it would be for this occasion only and that the
troops would be withdrawn when they had accom
plished their purpose.
It was said that the request for permission to send
the American troops into Mexico probably would go
forward to General Carrauza before tonight.
